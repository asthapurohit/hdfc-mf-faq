## Factual Queries (Expected: Answer + Source)

### Q1: What is expense ratio of HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?
**A:** The expense ratio of HDFC Flexi Cap Fund is 0.77% per annum 
for the Direct Plan and 1.59% per annum for the Regular Plan. 
The TER is subject to change and the latest information can be 
found at hdfcfund.com/investor-services/total-expense-ratio.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/investor-services/total-expense-ratio  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q2: What is lock-in period for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
**A:** The HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund has a mandatory lock-in period 
of 3 years from the date of allotment of each SIP instalment. 
Units cannot be redeemed before 3 years. It is eligible for tax 
deduction up to Rs 1.5 lakh under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-elss-tax-saver/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q3: How do I download my capital gains statement on Groww?
**A:** Open the Groww app, tap 'You' at the bottom right corner, 
select 'SIP & Reports', tap 'Capital Gain', choose the financial 
year, and tap 'Download'.  
**Source:** https://groww.in/blog/how-to-get-capital-gains-statement-for-mutual-fund-investments  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q4: What is exit load for HDFC Top 100 Fund?
**A:** HDFC Top 100 Fund charges an exit load of 1% if units are 
redeemed or switched out within 1 year from the date of allotment. 
No exit load is applicable after 1 year.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-top-100-fund/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q5: What is minimum SIP for HDFC Mid Cap Opportunities Fund?
**A:** The minimum SIP amount for HDFC Mid Cap Opportunities Fund 
is Rs 100. The minimum lumpsum investment is also Rs 100. 
Additional purchases also require a minimum of Rs 100.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-mid-cap-fund/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q6: What is benchmark of HDFC Nifty 50 Index Fund?
**A:** The benchmark index of HDFC Nifty 50 Index Fund is the 
NIFTY 50 Total Returns Index (TRI). The additional benchmark 
is BSE SENSEX TRI. The fund passively tracks the Nifty 50 
subject to tracking error.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-nifty-50-index-fund/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q7: What is riskometer rating of HDFC Small Cap Fund?
**A:** HDFC Small Cap Fund is rated Very High risk as per SEBI's 
riskometer. The fund invests predominantly in small-cap companies 
ranked 251st and below by market capitalisation.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-small-cap-fund/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19

---

### Q8: What is exit load for HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund?
**A:** For HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund, 15% of units may be 
redeemed without any exit load from the date of allotment. 
For redemptions beyond this limit, 1% exit load applies if 
redeemed within 1 year. No exit load applies after 1 year.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/explore/mutual-funds/hdfc-balanced-advantage-fund/direct  
**Last updated from sources:** 2026-04-19
